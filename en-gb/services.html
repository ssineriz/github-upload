    What we can offer

    We offer a wide range of services to meet the needs of every type of company.

    Architecture design

    Seashell offers the support of a solution architect to those who prefer to manage their project independently, and want to guarantee a solid architecture, starting on the right foot.

    Project support

    If your project is going through a critical phase, Seashell puts its best resources at your disposal to help you identify obstacles and guide you in solving them.

    Integration

    With a consolidated experience in data integration and Web APIs, Seashell can build and connect the most common data interfaces on microservices and ESB (Enterprise Service Bus), whether Cloud or Ground, addressing all issues related to security, robustness and performance.

    Data science

    Analytics: retrieval, correlation, interpretation and display of trends in a set of data.
    Big data: management and distributed database queries (BigQuery, DWH, Data Lake, Data Mart).
    Machine learning: design, training and tuning of ML models (DNN, LSTM, RNN).

    Offshore development

    You can also trust us to create well-defined modules within a project, leaving the burden of development to us. We have an excellent quality-to-price ratio and we guarantee to respect short deadlines.

    Advice

    To help you find answers to your technological needs, take advantage of an expert at a critical stage or simply get a second opinion, do not hesitate to contact us.
