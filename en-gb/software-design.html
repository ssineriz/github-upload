    The phases of a project iteration

    Every project is special. Proper methodology ensures its success.

    01

    Requirements assessment

    Together with the customer, we set out the list of requirements, categorized by impact (ROI) and priority (required vs. optional).

    02

    Analysis and feasibility

    Our analysts identify any problems that could arise in the requirements, in terms of load, safety, resources, time and costs.

    03

    Architecture design

    Our software and system architects design the structure of the solution and set the foundations of the entire project: technologies, systems, scope, rules, tools, development methods and documentation.

    04

    Solution development

    This phase generally covers 40-50% of the time required to complete the entire project and is carried out in "flexible" mode: short development iterations (sprints) and weekly or fortnightly meetings with the client and the team.

    05

    Test

    The following tests are fundamental to guarantee the quality of the solution:
    • Unit test: all the functions identified in the requirements pass a series of tests, both positive and negative. 
    • System test: high-load, integration and safety situations are simulated.
    • User acceptance test: final check performed by end users.

    06

    Publication

    Once the tests are completed and the requirements coverage has been met, the system is ready for production. Often it is a switch-off from a previous system. The following deliverable are provided to the customer: 
    • design of the architecture
    • detailed analysis of the solution implemented
    • test book and coverage of the requirements
    • operating manuals
    • user manual

    07

    Post-release support

    All our turn-key projects are covered by warranty. Our Application Maintenance services are available if you wish to follow the product throughout its life cycle.
